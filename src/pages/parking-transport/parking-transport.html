COACH TRANSFERS

Transfer service via coach shuttle can be purchased for the pre and post cruise arrangements. Purchase of transfer ticker can be made through your travel agent, cruise line or directly onboard. Please check with your cruise line for further details.

HOTEL AND OFF SITE PARKING SHUTTLES

Pick-up zones A, B, C, D are clearly marked across the main building and are designated pick up areas for Hotels and Off site Parking Shuttles.

TAXI

Taxi are available in the designated taxi lot across the parking garage. Taxi rates are available with the dispatcher at the taxi stand.

PRIVATE CARS

Pick-up Zones 6 - 10 are clearly marked along the main building and there are designated parking areas for private cars.
